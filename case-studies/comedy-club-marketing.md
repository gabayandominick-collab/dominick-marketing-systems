# 🎤 Case Study: Scaling Ticket Sales for 9 Comedy Clubs & 5 Venues

## Overview

I managed digital marketing campaigns for a multi-venue entertainment group, handling **9 comedy clubs and 5 bar/lounge venues**.

The goal was to **increase ticket sales, improve ad performance, and build a repeatable system** for promoting shows consistently.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Increase ticket sales for weekly shows and events
- Improve return on ad spend (ROAS)
- Build a scalable and repeatable marketing system
- Reduce manual follow-ups through automation

---

## 💰 Ad Spend Managed

- Google Ads: ~$70,000/month  
- Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram): ~$50,000/month  
- Total: **$100,000+ monthly ad spend**

---

## ⚙️ Strategy & Execution

### 1. Paid Ads System (Google + Meta)

- Launched targeted campaigns for each venue and event
- Segmented audiences by:
  - Location
  - Interests (comedy fans, nightlife, events)
  - Past engagement

- Created multiple ad variations:
  - Video creatives
  - Event posters
  - Short-form content

---

### 2. Retargeting Funnel

- Built retargeting campaigns for:
  - Website visitors
  - Video viewers
  - Engaged social media users

- Focused on:
  - Reminder ads
  - Urgency (limited seats, upcoming shows)
  - Social proof

---

### 3. Funnel & Conversion Optimization

- Improved landing pages for:
  - Faster loading
  - Clear CTAs (Buy Tickets)
  - Mobile optimization

- Reduced drop-off by simplifying the purchase flow

---

### 4. CRM & Automation

- Implemented follow-up systems using CRM tools
- Automated:
  - Lead responses
  - Event reminders
  - Re-engagement campaigns

- Reduced manual workload while improving response time

---

### 5. Reporting & Optimization

- Built dashboards using Looker Studio
- Tracked:
  - Cost per ticket
  - Conversion rates
  - Campaign performance

- Continuously optimized:
  - Audience targeting
  - Ad creatives
  - Budget allocation

---

## 📊 Results

- Managed and optimized **$100K+ monthly ad spend**
- Improved campaign efficiency through structured testing
- Increased ticket sales consistency across multiple venues
- Built a repeatable marketing system for ongoing promotions
- Reduced reliance on manual processes through automation

---

## 🧠 Key Takeaways

- Retargeting is critical for event-based businesses
- Consistent testing of creatives improves performance
- Structured systems outperform one-off campaigns
- Automation increases efficiency and scalability

---

## 🚀 What Makes This Different

Instead of running isolated campaigns, I built a **complete marketing system** that includes:

- Paid ads (Google + Meta)
- Retargeting funnels
- CRM automation
- Performance tracking

This allowed the business to scale across multiple venues while maintaining consistent results.
